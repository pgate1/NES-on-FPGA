      NES on FPGA feat. TangNano20K
                                         by pgate1

- Overview

Implemented NES mapper 0,1,2,3,4,73.
Mario1,3, DQ1,2,3,4, FF1,2,3, Kirby of the stars, Salamanda, etc.

- Usage

Write the ROM to the microSD card and insert it into the Tang Nano 20K.
Supports microSD cards of 32MB to 2GB formatted in FAT16.

After connecting Tang Nano 20K and PC with USB cable,
Write NES.fs in SRAM mode.

- Control

The Start and A buttons are assigned to the S1 button.
S2 button is reset.
