      NES on FPGA feat. TangNano4K
                                         by pgate1

- Overview

Implemented NES mapper 0.
Super Mario Bros., Wrecking Crew, etc.

- Usage

After connecting Tang Nano 4K and PC with a USB cable, write NES.fs in SRAM mode.
Send the ROM with FT_Write via FTDI driver.

- Control

The Start and A buttons are assigned to the buttons to the right of the USB connector.
The left button is reset.
